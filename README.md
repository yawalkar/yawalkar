### Hi, I'm Nitin 👋

I build automation and AI infrastructure for WordPress — and a few things outside it.

I'm the founder of **[InfiWebs Pvt. Ltd.](https://www.infiwebs.com)**, a small studio in Pune, India, where we ship products at the intersection of WordPress, workflow automation, and AI. I've been writing WordPress plugins since 2013; these days, most of my time goes into **[FlowMattic](https://flowmattic.com)** and pushing MCP-native AI deeper into the WordPress ecosystem.

---

#### 🛠 What I'm building

- **[FlowMattic](https://flowmattic.com)** — Self-hosted WordPress workflow automation with 450+ integrations, AI Agents, and full MCP Server/Client support. Think Zapier/Make/n8n, without the recurring SaaS bill.
- **[FlowTick](https://flowtick.io)** — WhatsApp Business inbox and chatbot SaaS ( Coming soon ).
- **[GetMCP](https://getmcp.com)** — A WordPress plugin that lets any SaaS expose its APIs as native MCP tools for any MCP-compatible AI client — regardless of the host stack.
- **[TypeMasti](https://typemasti.com)** — A PWA that teaches kids typing, vocabulary, and numbers. Originally built for my 5-year-old; now used by quite a few more.
